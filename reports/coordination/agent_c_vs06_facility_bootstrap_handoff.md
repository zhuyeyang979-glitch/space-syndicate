## Lessons for other agents

- **Invariant:** rank-I facility acquisition costs cash 4, but facility play costs zero assets.

- **Stable API:** consume `machine.purchase_cash`, `machine.asset_cost`, `machine.industry_id`, and `effect_payload.facility_kind`; never infer cost from names or player text.
- **Test oracle:** enumerate the exact 16 families × four ranks and validate type-specific capacity monotonicity.
- **Integration trap:** a rank-I fixture no longer proves colored/generic asset debit or compensation.
- **Reusable pattern:** move nonzero payment/rollback coverage to rank II while retaining rank I as the zero-asset bootstrap case.
- **Stale evidence:** Agent A's facility unlock balance assertions still encode the former one-asset fee.
